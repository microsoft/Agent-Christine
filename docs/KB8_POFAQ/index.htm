General Questions:

1.      Question: Can a customer change from a public plan to a private plan?

Answer: Yes, if the private plan has the same billing terms as the public plan.

 

2.      Question: As an ISV, how to handle upsell on a currently accepted private offer?

Answer: Create supplemental private offers based on a different plan, use v2 option if leveraging same public plan, or cancel and create new private offers for future years.

3.      Question: What happens if a partner has created two overlapping private offers for the same product and customer?

Answer: The system is expected to pick the lowest price for the plan if there are more than one active private offers.

4.      Question: Is there a way to get the 50% discount on the agency fee for private plans?

Answer: No, there is no discount for private plans. 

5.      Question: Can a customer back out of an accepted private offer?

Answer: There is no reversing or canceling an accepted private offer. The customer can elect not to purchase, or the partner can submit a support ticket to cancel the accepted private offer.

6.      Question: Are private offers supported in the Azure Government Marketplace?Answer: No, private offers are not supported in the Azure Government Marketplace.

7.      Question: Is there a way to see the acceptance and activation of a private plan?Answer: Private plans have no indicator of acceptance or subscription in Partner Center. For private offer, partners would receive email updates as well as status in Partner Center.

8.      Question: Is there an audit trail available in Partner Center for partners to see who/when purchased a private offer?

Answer: Insights reports show who purchased it but not who accepted it.

9.      Question: What happens to active private offers if a partner stops selling one of their public plans?

Answer: The price from the accepted date of the contract will remain, but current private offers that have been accepted but not purchased will be blocked. 

10. Question: What happens if a partner has created two overlapping private offers for the same product and customer?

Answer: The system is expected to pick the lowest price for the plan if there are more than one active private offers. 

11. Question: is the private offer and private plan the same?

Answer: No, private offer and private plan are different.
